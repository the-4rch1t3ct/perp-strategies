# Memecoin Correlation Study

## Overview

Analysis of cross-asset correlations in memecoin perpetual futures markets to inform portfolio construction and risk management.

## Methodology

- **Returns**: Log returns calculated from 1-hour candles
- **Correlation Window**: Rolling 168-hour (1 week) correlation
- **Assets Analyzed**: Top 20 memecoins by volume

## Key Findings

### Correlation Clusters

1. **High Correlation Group** (>0.7):
   - DOGE, SHIB, FLOKI (dog-themed memecoins)
   - PEPE, BONK (frog-themed memecoins)
   - WIF, BRETT (Solana ecosystem memecoins)

2. **Moderate Correlation** (0.4-0.7):
   - Cross-cluster correlations
   - Market-wide risk-on/risk-off periods

3. **Low Correlation** (<0.4):
   - Newer/lesser-known memecoins
   - Independent price action

### Dynamic Correlations

- **Bull Markets**: Correlations increase (all memecoins move together)
- **Bear Markets**: Correlations decrease (divergence)
- **Volatility Spikes**: Correlations spike temporarily (cascade effects)

## Portfolio Construction Rules

1. **Max Correlation Limit**: No more than 2 positions with >0.7 correlation
2. **Diversification**: Spread across different memecoin categories
3. **Dynamic Adjustment**: Reduce correlated positions during high volatility

## Risk Implications

- **Portfolio Risk**: High correlation = higher portfolio volatility
- **Liquidation Risk**: Correlated positions can liquidate simultaneously
- **Diversification Benefit**: Low correlation = better risk-adjusted returns
